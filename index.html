Top 3 Radiohead Albums

Hail to the Thief

Sometimes considered long and bloated, HttT is filled to the brim with incredible soundscapes and melodies. It is something of a "grower", so repeat listens are a must.

Kid A

At the time of its release, Kid A was a jarring departure from their established, award winning sound. While it was critically acclaimed, some fans didn't know what to think at first.

A Moon Shaped Pool

After 2 decades of making music, Radiohead released their 8th album to momumental acclaim. Radiohead's still got it. String orchestrations are a mainstay here, and the haunting, somber melodies will bring you to tears.
